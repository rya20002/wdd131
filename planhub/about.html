about the site

Why PlanHub exists

PlanHub is a personal lifestyle dashboard designed to help users organize different areas of life in one structured space. It gives users a place to manage recipes, track goals, save favorite activities, and build custom lists in a clean and visually appealing interface.

Purpose

PlanHub was created to bring everyday planning into one clear system. Instead of using several separate apps or scattered notes, users can keep practical life content in one organized place.

Audience

The intended audience is college students and young adults who want a simple and organized way to manage daily life. The site is made for users who value productivity, clarity, and aesthetic design.

Dynamic Features

The site uses JavaScript for recipe filtering, a to-do list, a custom list builder, a goal tracker, quick stats, and a quote generator. Users can add, remove, complete, and update content directly on the page.

Design Direction

The design follows a calm, organized, and minimal aesthetic. It uses soft neutral colors, readable typography, rounded cards, and a responsive layout that works well on both mobile and desktop screens.

design style

Clean, calm, and structured

The layout is built to feel polished but simple. The goal is to make content easy to read, useful to interact with, and visually consistent with the site plan.
